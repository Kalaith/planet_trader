# Future Improvements - Planet Trader

## Trading & Economics
1. **Dynamic Market Simulation**: Advanced economic modeling with supply/demand affecting prices across star systems
2. **Trade Route Optimization**: AI-assisted route planning with fuel costs, piracy risks, and profit calculations
3. **Futures Trading**: Contract-based trading for future delivery of goods with price speculation
4. **Commodity Exchanges**: Real-time commodity exchanges with dynamic AI-driven market activity
5. **Economic Warfare**: Corporate espionage, market manipulation, and trade embargo mechanics

6. **Procedural Galaxy Generation**: Massive, explorable galaxy with unique star systems and trade opportunities
7. **Ancient Alien Artifacts**: Discover lost technologies that provide trading advantages or new ship capabilities

9. **First Contact Protocols**: Discover new alien species and establish trade relationships
10. **Dangerous Space**: Navigate black holes, asteroid fields, and cosmic phenomena that affect trade routes
